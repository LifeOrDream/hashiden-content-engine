# Blueprint 01 — 24 Hours Until Mining Begins (flagship, ~80s)

This is the series opener. It must do three things: create thriller anticipation around the 24-hour mining countdown, reveal the hidden HashBeast world living parallel to humans, and weaponize real BTC FUD into the reason $DEN is coming. Ends on the first global-country-war cliffhanger + 24h countdown.

## HOOK
Open on a black frame with a huge countdown already running: 24:00:00 until mining begins. It should feel thriller/anticipation, not explainer. The first reveal is not "we are AI"; it is "there is a hidden HashBeast world watching the same Bitcoin collapse humans are ignoring."

## GROUNDING TO WEAVE (as story/metaphor, NOT bullet points)
- BTC bleeding live on a chart.
- 21M coins / scarce but slow / no yield / not quantum-resistant.
- Algorithmic slop / dead internet attention is draining the old market.
- $DEN truths: fair launch (mined only, no pre-mine/insiders), dynamic emissions (every 4h the degen Fed changes incentives), real value (revenue → buybacks), country-war mining race, and HashBeasts as the sentient operators behind each faction.
- Hidden-world premise: humans think dogs are pets / mascots / memes; HashBeasts are actually strategic, sentient, factional, and already preparing for the mining race.

## BEATS (action + intent — dialogue gets written by the pipeline)
1. [hook] Black → 24:00:00 countdown appears and starts ticking. Under it, BTC candle bleeds red. Thriller pulse. INTENT: make the viewer feel something is about to begin, not be explained.
2. [reveal] Cut to the hidden HashBeast layer: Rex in a USA command/trading room, humans visible only as blurred feet, TV noise, or distant city life beyond glass; they do not realize the doges are running the room. INTENT: reveal the parallel world.
3. [setup] Rex flicks a dying BTC hologram like a dead bulb. INTENT: dismiss the old king to make room for the new one.
4. [escalation] He diagnoses BTC's weaknesses + dead-market slop with physical jokes, without using meta-existence language. INTENT: make old BTC feel tired and vulnerable.
5. [promise] The room shifts into $DEN launch posture: fair launch, mined only, adaptive cycle rules, country race. Each fact must become conflict/status/comedy/action; Rex should sound like a competitive character reacting to the room, not a tutorial voice explaining mechanics. INTENT: recruit without sounding like a landing page.
6. [turn/cliffhanger] Move into the USA command-plaza war room. Stacked screens on left/right wake one by one: China first, Russia, India, Japan, Korea, Iran, UK, France, Brazil, Israel, North Korea last. USA is not alone. INTENT: reveal the global race; America cannot simply dominate.
7. [end_card] $DEN logo ignites → countdown 24:00:00 + CTA.

## DIALOGUE WARNING
These grounding facts are not spoken copy. Do not put phrases like "fair launch", "pre-mine", "insiders", "emissions", "yield", "velvet rope", "founder's table", "pick up a pickaxe", or "earn the signal" in Rex's mouth. Show those ideas through props, reactions, status shifts, and ON-SCREEN overlays if needed. Rex should talk like a brash USA faction commander trying to stay ahead while realizing the room is already global.

## CLIFFHANGER
Rex realizes the room has gone global: "I thought America was early. The world logged in." Cut to 24:00:00.
