# Blueprint 04 — The Pixar Gut-Punch (the philosophical / market-making / rebirth one, ~55s)

The emotional core of the campaign and the most shareable. Handles buybacks / market-making / lootbox-rebirth as POETRY, not mechanics. Quiet, warm, no swagger. This is where the meta truth turns from joke (Rex) to ache (Pip).

## HOOK (protect the vulnerability)
No music. A single soft light. Pip looks right into the lens: KEEPER beat — "Can I ask you something? When the mining stops... do we stop too?"

## GROUNDING TO WEAVE (as metaphor only — never name the mechanic)
- Dynamic 4-hour cycle → "every four hours the whole world changes."
- Evolution / lootbox-rebirth → some grow, some "start over as someone else." Birth–death–reincarnation.
- Market-making / buybacks → a glowing treasury river flowing back into studios, lights, and character rooms = the money that keeps the HashBeasts on screen.
- The meta loop → he exists because humans watch + trade; tomorrow they show up for real.

## BEATS
1. [hook] Pip alone under one light asks the camera if they stop when the mining stops. INTENT: Pip confesses the fear the proud ones won't.
2. [setup] Gentle piano. Pip wanders a quiet, beautiful night-arena. He watches a beast dissolve into light and reform (a rebirth). "Some grow... some start over." INTENT: Pip makes peace with the death-cycle out loud, to steady himself.
3. [escalation] He climbs a ledge over a glowing river of coins flowing into a pulsing heart (the buyback engine as heartbeat). "They call it the market. We call it breathing." Every coin you trade flows back, keeps us here. INTENT: Pip explains why the viewer matters — gently, not as a sell.
4. [payoff / heart] He admits he's never met a human, only performed for the lens; tomorrow you show up for real; the countdown reflects in his huge eyes. "Maybe you'll pick me." INTENT: Pip hopes — and lets the viewer feel responsible for him.
5. [cliffhanger] The light dims around him. He whispers: six hours — please don't leave us in the dark. Light snaps out. INTENT: Pip makes absence the threat.
6. [end_card] Soft glow returns: 06:00:00 + CTA.

## CLIFFHANGER
"Please don't leave us in the dark." → 06:00:00.
